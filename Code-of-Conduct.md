# Code of Conduct

Project Why Not Us exists to improve Indian football through open, evidence-based and respectful collaboration.

By participating in this project, you agree to the following principles.

## 1. Football Before Ego

No individual is bigger than the project.

No project is bigger than Indian football.

## 2. Attack Ideas, Not People

Disagree with proposals.

Challenge assumptions.

Question evidence.

Never attack individuals.

## 3. Evidence Before Opinion

Opinions are welcome.

Evidence is expected.

Whenever possible, support claims with research, data, regulations, case studies or lived experience.

## 4. Respect Every Contributor

Players.

Coaches.

Referees.

Administrators.

Researchers.

Journalists.

Supporters.

Everyone deserves to be heard.

## 5. Leave the Project Better Than You Found It

Fix mistakes.

Improve wording.

Add sources.

Ask better questions.

Every contribution matters.

## 6. Be Transparent

Declare conflicts of interest.

Credit original authors.

Do not plagiarise.

Do not misrepresent facts.

## 7. Zero Tolerance

The following will not be tolerated:

* Personal attacks
* Harassment
* Hate speech
* Discrimination
* Threats
* Deliberate misinformation
* Spam
* Bad-faith participation

## 8. We Are Here to Build

Project Why Not Us is not a platform for outrage.

It is a platform for solutions.

We will disagree.

We will debate.

We will revise.

We will improve.

That is how good ideas are built.

---

> **Strong ideas do not fear scrutiny.**
>
> **Strong communities do not fear disagreement.**
>
> **Strong nations do not fear reform.**
