# Project-Why-Not-Us
Home of the National Football Development Code (NFDC) and Project Why Not Us.

Project Why Not Us is a community driven initiative dedicated to building an evidence based future for Indian football through open collaboration, research and public participation.

Our flagship initiative is the **National Football Development Code (NFDC)**, an evolving policy framework that seeks to consolidate research, governance, law, football development and international best practices into a single living document.

This repository is not just a document.

It is a movement.

---

# Why does this exist?

Every football fan has an opinion.

Few have a platform to convert those opinions into structured proposals.

Project Why Not Us exists to change that.

Rather than asking,

> "Who should we blame?"

we ask,

> "How do we build something better?"

---

# What is the National Football Development Code?

The National Football Development Code (NFDC) is an open policy framework for Indian football.

It is **not**

- a law
- a Government policy
- an AIFF regulation

Instead, it is intended to become a collaborative blueprint for the long term development of Indian football.

The NFDC attempts to bring together:

- governance
- grassroots football
- youth development
- coaching
- scouting
- infrastructure
- finance
- sports science
- women's football
- international pathways
- football law
- model policies
- implementation frameworks
- monitoring and evaluation

into one coherent framework.
